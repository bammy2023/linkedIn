The movement emphasizing free markets to reduce poverty has found strong expression in the bottom of the pyramid approach in recent years. It views the poor as flexible and creative entrepreneurs and value-awake consumers. This imaginary view of the poor harms the poor in two ways. First, it results in too little stress on legal, regulatory and social mechanisms to protect the poor who are vulnerable consumers. More importantly, it grossly a special critical role and responsibility of the state in poverty reduction

The Bottom of the Pyramid (BOP)

It is a socio-economic concept that allows us to group that vast segment - in excess of about four billion - of the worlds poorest citizens constituting an invisible and unserved market blocked by challenging barriers that prevent them from realising their human potential for their own benefit, those of their families, and that of society's at larger

It's a creative and productive potential as part of an inclusive capitalist system, free of paternalism toward the poor.

Need To understand their Psychology clearly

The thing about most rich people is, they have to do almost everything 'rich people style'. Part of being super rich comes with the satisfaction that you get to have your name on rare, expensive, luxury items

Time and money are interchangeable. You can always save one by spending more of the other. The rich have always had more money than time.

 Put your product where they can see it

"In the last 30 years or so, as people have become increasingly isolated from their neighbours, a stream of magazines and television shows celebrating the parties of the rich has fostered a whole new level of desire across class groups..."

The fastest growing category in the world of marketing is called "product placement." For a price, your product can be written into the script of a TV show or major motion picture. Do you remember the year James Bond began driving a new BMW convertible? Or the Seinfeld episode where George buys a cashmere sweater in a trendy boutique? These things don't happen by accident. And when your product is on the show instead of in an ad, you're immune from TiVo fast-forwarding.

Target through copy.

In your product category, what are the phrases the rich will likely type into a search engine? Have you seeded these phrases throughout your website? Pre-purchase research is increasingly being done online instead of in the store. So think of your site as a half step between the homes of the rich and your front door. How strong is the magnetism of your site?

Make them feel comforts of your product

After you have identified your Rich customers, youll need to develop the right marketing messages for Rich customers. Your messaging should communicate the ideal end result or end feeling of using your products and services. Rich People dont buy your products and services because of the features alone; they buy them because of the benefits and advantages that those features will provide and how the product or services will make them feel.

Establish trust and credibility

When marketing to high-end customers, it is important to establish trust and credibility to minimize resistance in the sales process. Some highly effective ways to establish trust with high-end prospects include the use of media, credible introductions and endorsements from key influencers, testimonials, and industry data.
